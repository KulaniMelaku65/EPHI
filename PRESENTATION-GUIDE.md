# EPHI Training Management System
## Executive Summary & Presentation Guide

---

## 🎯 **What is This System?**

A complete web-based platform for the Ethiopian Public Health Institute to manage all training programs, track healthcare worker development, and analyze capacity building efforts across Ethiopia.

---

## ✨ **Key Features for EPHI**

### **For Administrators**
- ✅ Track all 248+ trainings across 13 regions
- ✅ Monitor 3,421+ healthcare workers trained
- ✅ Manage 67+ trainers and their performance
- ✅ Approve funding requests with full audit trail
- ✅ Generate reports for Ministry of Health
- ✅ Real-time dashboards with charts and analytics

### **For Trainers**
- ✅ Create training sessions with location selection
- ✅ Manage participant registrations
- ✅ Submit funding requests online
- ✅ View training history and ratings
- ✅ Automatic email notifications to trainees

### **For Trainees (Healthcare Workers)**
- ✅ Browse available trainings by region/topic
- ✅ Register online with instant confirmation
- ✅ Download digital certificates
- ✅ Track continuing education credits
- ✅ View upcoming training calendar

### **For External Partners (WHO, CDC, etc.)**
- ✅ View public training statistics
- ✅ Filter data by region/topic/date
- ✅ Export reports for donor reporting
- ✅ No login required for public data

## 📊 **What Data is Tracked?**

### **Training Information**
- Topic and duration (3-7 days typically)
- Trainer name and qualifications
- Location (health facility + region + woreda)
- Start and end dates
- Number of participants
- Registration status

### **Participant Details**
- Full name and contact information
- Health facility and position
- Region and woreda
- Years of experience
- Education level
- Previous trainings attended

### **Performance Metrics**
- Completion rates by region
- Trainer ratings (1-5 stars)
- Pre-test and post-test scores
- Certificate issuance
- Budget utilization

### **Funding Information**
- Requested amount (in ETB)
- Number of participants
- Justification
- Approval status
- Actual spending

## 📧 **Automated Email Notifications**

The system automatically sends emails for:

1. **Registration Confirmation**
   - Sent to trainee immediately
   - Includes training details
   - Location and dates
   - What to bring

2. **Training Reminder**
   - Sent 7 days before training
   - Sent 1 day before training
   - Includes directions

3. **Certificate Ready**
   - Sent when certificate is generated
   - Includes download link
   - Certificate number for verification

4. **Funding Request Status**
   - Approval notification
   - Rejection with reason
   - Disbursement confirmation

## 🔒 **Data Security & Privacy**

### **Security Features**
- Encrypted passwords (bcrypt hashing)
- SSL/HTTPS for all connections
- Session timeout after 30 minutes
- Role-based access control
- Activity logging and audit trail

### **Privacy Protection**
- External users see NO personal data
- Trainees see only their own data
- Trainers see only their sessions
- Admins have full access (EPHI staff only)

### **Data Ownership**
- All data stored in Ethiopia (local hosting)
- EPHI owns all data
- Can export anytime
- No vendor lock-in

## ❓ **Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: Do we need to install anything?**
A: No! Just open a web browser.

**Q: What if internet goes down?**
A: System caches recent data. Users can still view (but not save new data).

**Q: Can we customize the system?**
A: Yes! Everything can be modified to EPHI needs.

**Q: What about data migration?**
A: We can import existing Excel data easily.

**Q: Is training provided?**
A: Yes! User guides + 2-day training for admins.

**Q: What about technical support?**
A: Email/phone support during business hours.

**Q: Can we integrate with DHIS2?**
A: Yes! API available for integration.

**Q: What happens if hosting company fails?**
A: Your data is backed up. Can move to another host in 1 day.
